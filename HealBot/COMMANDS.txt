hb disable [cmd]
===============
na		:	Disables status removal
cure	:	Disables all single cures
curaga 	:	Disables all curagas
buffs	:	Disables all buffing (Haste, Refresh, etc.)
debuff	:	Disables all debuffing (Dia, Bio, etc.)
spam	:	Disables spamming ('//hb help' to see more info on spam)
ws		: 	Disables weaponskilling
